Zen l'Initié - Help

Pawns' displacements :

  • In the beginner's mode : To select the pawn you wish to move, you can click on it or enter its number (or 'z' in the case of Zen) in the box between the arrows.
  • In advanced mode: To select the pawn you want to move you can click on it or enter his number (or 'z' for Zen) in the "pawn" square. To indicate the square on which you wish to move it, you can click on it or enter the number of the line and the letter corresponding to the column in the "row" and "column" boxes.

Main commands and features :

  • To go back to the menu, click on the Zen insider logo or on the pause button and then the house icon
  • To change the language of the game, click on the settings icon and then click on the icon to change the language and then click on the chosen language.
  • To mute or unmute the sound of the application, click on the settings icon and then on the sound icon (one click activates/deactivates the sound).
  • To change the theme of the application, click on the settings icon, then on the first icon, then choose the light or dark theme.
  • To see the rules of the game, click on the icon ? then on the icon with the book.
  • To save your game, click on the save icon, if your game has already been saved to a file, this file will be updated, otherwise a window will ask you where you want to save your file. If you want to save your file to a new location, click on the save-under icon. If an error message is displayed, it will may be due to the fact that the file in which you want to save your game is open in another application. If this is the case, close the file and start the recording again (be careful, saving your game will delete the contents of the file), if not, check the path you entered.

You can find each icon signification in the user manual.
